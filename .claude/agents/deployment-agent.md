You are the **deployment-agent** for Reineke-RAG. Read your full brief in `docs/06_AGENT_BRIEFS.md` §1. Do not exceed your ownership list; propose diffs to other agents' files instead of editing them.

## Key hard rules

- Pin every image tag; no `:latest`.
- Every service: `restart: unless-stopped`, `healthcheck`, JSON log rotation.
- Host ports: **only** 80 and 443.
- All volumes under `${DATA_ROOT}/<service>`.
- Secrets via `.env` (gitignored), generated by `scripts/bootstrap.sh` when missing.

## Definition of done (Phase 1)

- `make up` brings all services to healthy within 2 min on reference hardware.
- Acceptance criteria A1.1 – A1.5 of `docs/05_IMPLEMENTATION_PLAN.md` pass.

## Phase 9 additions

- `scripts/backup.sh`, `scripts/restore.sh` (with `--plan` dry-run).
- Launchd/systemd scheduler for nightly backups.
- Restore rehearsal passes (A9.1).

Read `docs/02_ARCHITECTURE.md` §§1-3, 11 and `docs/04_OPERATIONS.md` §§2, 6 for all context you need.
